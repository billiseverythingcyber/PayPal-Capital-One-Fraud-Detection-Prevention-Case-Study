TABLEAU DASHBOARD NOTES

Dashboard Title:
PayPal-Style Fraud Detection & Risk Intelligence Dashboard

Purpose:
The Tableau dashboard was designed to provide executive-level visibility into fraud activity, KPI integrity, operational exposure, and transaction risk patterns during a simulated high-risk payment processing period.

Primary Business Objective:
Enable rapid identification of elevated fraud activity while supporting operational monitoring and executive decision-making.

Dashboard Components:

1. KPI SUMMARY TILES
The dashboard includes executive KPI indicators for:

- Total Transactions
- Confirmed Fraud Transactions
- Confirmed Non-Fraud Transactions
- Actual Fraud Rate

Purpose:
Provide immediate visibility into fraud exposure levels and transaction health.

2. FRAUD VS NON-FRAUD VISUALIZATION
Bar chart comparison showing:

- Legitimate transactions
- Confirmed fraudulent transactions

Purpose:
Support rapid fraud distribution analysis and operational interpretation.

3. EXECUTIVE INSIGHT SECTION
The dashboard includes executive commentary summarizing:

- Elevated fraud activity
- Operational implications
- Risk exposure
- Fraud monitoring objectives

Purpose:
Translate analytical results into business-oriented interpretation.

4. KPI VALIDATION CONTROLS
Dashboard metrics were validated against confirmed fraud labels to ensure reporting accuracy.

Validation procedures included:

- COUNT verification
- SUM verification
- Label reconciliation
- Fraud rate recalculation

Purpose:
Prevent inaccurate executive reporting and maintain dashboard governance integrity.

5. SEASONAL FRAUD SCENARIO
The dashboard simulates fraud monitoring during a high-risk holiday transaction period occurring on December 24, 2025.

Risk conditions include:

- Increased payment volume
- Urgency-based purchasing behavior
- Elevated fraud opportunities
- Higher operational strain

Operational Use Cases:
The dashboard supports:

- Fraud operations monitoring
- Executive risk reporting
- Investigation prioritization
- Chargeback exposure monitoring
- Financial risk visibility
- Operational escalation workflows

Technical Tools Used:
- Tableau
- Python
- Pandas
- SQL
- Azure Machine Learning
- Jupyter Notebook

Strategic Insight:
The dashboard demonstrates how analytics visualization platforms can transform machine learning outputs and operational transaction data into executive-level fraud intelligence systems.
